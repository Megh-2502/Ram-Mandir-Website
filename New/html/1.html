Ram Mandir Darshan : Ticket, Time & Registration / Booking

Temple Darshan Timing:

7.00 AM to 11.30 AM – 02:00 PM to 07:00 PM

Jagaran / Shringar Aarti : 06:30 AM – One Day prior Advance Booking is possible depending upon the availability

Sandhya Aarti : 07:30 PM – Same-day booking is possible subject to availability

Note: For Aarti Passes, please reach the Camp Office at Shri Ram Janmabhoomi/Ram Mandir 30 minutes before the Aarti Time with a valid Government ID Proof, like Aadhar Card, Voter ID Card, PAN Card, etc…

Departure & Return Location

Maharishi Valmiki International Airport – Ayodhya

Key Details about Ram Mandir Darshan

  • Inauguration Start Date: January 16, 2024. This marks the beginning of a series of events leading up to the main consecration ceremony.
  • Lord Ram Lalla’s Consecration: Scheduled for January 22, 2024, this is the highlight of the inauguration, signifying the sanctification of the deity’s idol.

Aarti Pass Booking:

  • How to Book: Passes for the Aarti can be booked through two methods – online and offline. For online bookings, the official website of Shri Ram Janmbhoomi Teerth Kshetra is the primary platform.
  • Identification Requirement: Attendees must provide a valid government-issued ID during the booking process. This is essential for verification and security purposes.

Aarti Schedule:

  1. Shringar Aarti (Morning): Held at 06:30 AM, this Aarti involves the ceremonial adorning of the deity, marking the beginning of the day’s rituals.
  2. Bhog Aarti (Afternoon): Scheduled for 12:00 PM, this Aarti is performed during the offering of food to the deity.
  3. Sandhya Aarti (Evening): Taking place at 07:30 PM, this Aarti signifies the evening prayers and is an integral part of the day’s worship.

Pass Holder Information:

  • Attendance Limit: Due to security concerns, each Aarti will have a strict limit of 30 attendees. This ensures a safe and orderly environment for the ceremony.
  • Pass Requirement: Only individuals with valid passes will be granted entry to the Aarti sessions, emphasizing the importance of securing a pass beforehand.

Detailed Steps for Booking Aarti Passes:

  1. Website Visit: Begin by visiting the official website, srjbtkshetra.org, which serves as the primary platform for all information and bookings related to the event.
  2. Mobile Number & OTP Login: Enter your mobile number on the website and use the One-Time Password (OTP) sent to your phone for a secure login.
  3. Selecting Aarti: On the homepage, navigate to the ‘Aarti’ section where you can choose the specific Aarti you wish to attend.
  4. Providing Details: Enter necessary personal details like your name, address, photograph, and mobile number, which are crucial for the booking process.
  5. Pass Collection: After booking, remember to collect your Aarti pass from the designated counter at the temple on the day of your visit.

Accepted Documents for Verification:

  • List of Documents: Attendees can use any of the following for booking verification – Driver’s License, Voter ID, Aadhar Card, or Passport. It’s important to carry the same document to the temple that was used during booking.

Essential Instructions for Devotees:

  1. Children’s Entry: Children below 10 years of age do not require a separate pass, making it easier for families to attend.
  2. ID Proof Consistency: It’s crucial to bring the same ID proof to the temple that was used at the time of booking for verification purposes.
  3. Wheelchair Service: For those requiring wheelchair assistance, a nominal fee will be charged, ensuring accessibility for all devotees.
  4. Reminder Service: SRJBTK will send reminders via SMS/Email 24 hours before your booked Aarti, helping you to keep track of your schedule.
  5. Presence Confirmation: Devotees are required to confirm their attendance by logging into the website and updating their status under ‘Transaction History’.
  6. Pass Collection Point: Upon arrival at the temple, collect your pass from the Aarti pass counter located at the reporting location.

Final Note:

For more information and to book Aarti passes, devotees are encouraged to visit the official website. This guide aims to facilitate a smooth and fulfilling experience for all attendees at the historic Ram Mandir inauguration.
